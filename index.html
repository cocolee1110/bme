RESEARCH PROJECTS

Blinking Action on Cornea-on-a-Chip

Assessing the feasibility of developing an in-vitro cornea-on-a-chip model with recapitulates the corneal environemnt in vivo with the aim of using this model in the future as a platform to test antimicrobials against keratitis.

Microfluidics for high-content drug screening

Developing an automated high-content PMMA/PDMS microfluidic platform that measures hydraulic conductivity across HUVEC monolayer for glaucoma drug screening.

Biophysical phenotyping of red blood cells

Investigating the role of osmolarity in the cell suspending medium on RBCs deformability, by measuring its ability to perfuse in the artificial microvascular channels in a high-throughput microfluidic platform.
